Bridal wedding nail art style collection

2021-11-15

On the day of the wedding, every bride will put on her wedding dress, the most charming makeup and present the most exquisite state. However the bride hand maintenance is also very important, the majority of women should also pay attention to nail care. It is indispensable to add a grace and elegance to the girls who are about to get married on their wedding day. Next, let the blogger recommend some bridal wedding nail style pictures to the girls, I hope it can help you.

1.How to choose a wedding nail art style that suits you

Let's first consider the base length and shape of the nail art. If some brides have slightly short fingers and nails, then we can leave the nails long in advance, around 2-4mm. Then let the nail art staff help you to trim tiny squares or oval shape, which can slightly elongate the sense of the line of the hand. However, if you have slightly thicker fingers, it is not recommended to leave your nails long, as it will look a bit chunky. Oval nails will look longer because they are more slender and can generally achieve the elongated effect that girls want. But nails should never be left too long because they will look too sharp. In addition to the inconvenience of movement, it is also visually sharper. And the design of the square and round shape can also add a certain amount of femininity.

2.What kind of nail art shape suits me best?

The right shape for nail art varies from person to person. There are 5 types of nail art shapes: Oval, Square, Oval and Square, Round and Almond.
Square nails (suitable for people: wide nail bed, thin fingers)
Square round nails (suitable for people: narrow nail bed, long fingers)
Oval nails (for people with short nail beds and long hands)
Round (for people: short nail bed, do not like to keep long nails)

Nowadays, the more popular ones are oval and square, because they feel more like long fingers. But specifically which is better? It is better to take your own hand shape as a reference. Only with reference to the shape of the hand can you choose the right nail type, because of the way you can play the best complementary effect.

3.Bridal wedding nail art styles

Elegant and feminine nail art

Whether it is a Chinese or Western wedding, red is the primary choice for most brides. This tone of color makes the bride's hands appear whiter. Adding pearlescent silver and white accents to the rest of the fingers is also very good in the photo shoot. Not only does it look festive and also very noble, but it also highlights the femininity of the little family.

Pink girly nails art

If you are a bride who is playful and cute, we can choose some gradient colors and sweet styles of nail art. If you are a bride with short nails, don't worry, this nail art will not only make your short nails look cute, but also add a girly style to your long fingers. We can try fading from light pink to dark. Whether this manicure is a single color or you continue to add patterns, you will look pink and girly.

Simple and fresh nail art

With the development of society, more and more brides love the fresh style of nail art. The simple pink color nail is also the first choice of many brides. Its simple sense of lines under the base tone of powder blue and gray powder, and then combined with the European atmosphere full of flowers and patterns, it will look very delicate and atmospheric. When you do this nail art, the pure temperament and freshness at first glance undoubtedly under the pure white wedding dress.

Fantasy & Creative Nail Art

This nail art is the most recent internet celebrity nail art. It has a monochromatic nail polish as the base color, and then adding different hand-painted patterns of the same color will produce a mixed color base effect. Some of these starry shades of precious blue will give it a psychedelic starry sky effect as if the Milky Way is flowing. In addition to the visual sparkle and special, it will produce a dignified and gorgeous feeling when paired with a diamond ring in the photographer's shoot.

Morandi Color Nail Art

If you want to talk about what is the popular color for nail art recently, you can't fail to mention the color Morandi. This color with a gray scale itself carries a cool feeling and is perfect for mixing and matching with various colors. Whether it's a girly peach heart hand-painted color or a mature rhythm gray-brown color mix and match is very good.

Cute Girl Nail Art

Most of the brides who have just entered the marriage hall still retain a little bit of girlishness, because they have not yet fully assumed the role of wife and mother. Therefore, a cute girly bridal nail art is more likely to arouse your passion for fantasy and romance.
Pink nail art can be chosen from glassy or bean-sand color, which looks more clear and gentle. Or you can also choose stickers with star elements, while the powder blue color is more recognizable than the traditional star nails, which look like watercolor paintings.

Metallic Cool Nail Art

Not every bride likes a dreamy and romantic wedding theme. If you want a cool and individual look, you can totally go for a metallic nail art.

Metallic nail art makes you feel like a cool rocker. This nail art doesn't need to be too complicated, just put on a metallic patch and you're full of personality. If you think it's not enough, you can also try sprinkling.

Cartoon Style Nail Art

If the bride is more girly, then cartoon nail art must be very suitable. This cartoon style bridal red nail art uses a red bottom and a fortune cat graphic, which is quite a good symbol. Of course cartoon characters can also be Santa Claus, Wanted, etc. The bride can choose her own.
